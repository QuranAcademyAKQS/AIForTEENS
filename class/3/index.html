What are Neural Networks?

Key Concepts

Neural Networks are AI systems inspired by how human brains work. They consist of interconnected nodes (neurons) that process information in layers. Each connection has a weight that determines how much influence it has on the final decision.

Islamic Perspective

The human brain is one of Allah's most amazing creations. Neural networks attempt to mimic this divine design, showing how we can learn from Allah's creation to build beneficial technology.

Real-World Examples

Image recognition
Speech processing
Language translation

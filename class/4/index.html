AI in Healthcare

Key Concepts

AI is revolutionizing healthcare through medical imaging, drug discovery, personalized medicine, and patient care. AI can analyze medical images faster and more accurately than humans, helping doctors diagnose diseases earlier and more effectively.

Islamic Perspective

In Islam, preserving life and health is a sacred duty. AI in healthcare helps fulfill this obligation by improving medical care, making it more accessible, and saving lives through better diagnosis and treatment.
